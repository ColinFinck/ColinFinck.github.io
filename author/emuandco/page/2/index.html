Back from CLT 2010

by EmuandCo

March 15, 2010

The Chemnitz Linux-Days 2010 are over and all ReactOS developers have safely made their way home again. We would like to give a summary, and share our impressions with those who were unfortunately unable join us: Contrary to our expectations, the stall and the presentation were a wow. Especially for ReactOS Deutschland e.V., the new German non-profit organization supporting the project, it was a promising start. A lot of interested guests visited our stall and wished us success for the future.
